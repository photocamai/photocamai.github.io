Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how PhotoCamAI handles user information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how data is processed within the app.

Data Collection

PhotoCamAI does not collect, store, or share personally identifiable information. The app does not require users to create an account or provide personal details.

Photos and Media

Photos selected by the user are processed solely for the purpose of providing the app’s features. Photo processing is performed on the user’s device. Images are not uploaded, stored, or shared with third parties.

Subscriptions and Payments

All payments and subscriptions are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. PhotoCamAI does not have access to users’ payment information.

Third-Party Services

PhotoCamAI does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services that collect personal data.

Children’s Privacy

PhotoCamAI is not directed to children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy
